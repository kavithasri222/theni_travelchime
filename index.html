Places to visit in Theni for every vacation trips.

1.Megamalai,
sanctuary

Meghamalai in Theni district is the kind of hidden paradise that you read about in books, breathtaking scenes and an excellent opportunity to take a break from your everyday life in the lap of nature.

2.Kurangani,
Theni

This region remains an unexplored tourism spot. As we trek, we will be able to see Kurangani village below. The trek route is reasonable and some places are hard to climb.

3.Suruli Falls,
Theni

Suruli Falls is a beautiful waterfall in the Theni district of Tamil Nadu and one of the major tourist attractions of Theni as well. It is a perfect destination to get up close and personal with the nature and cool off in its pristine waters.

4.Seran's Fun Park,
Theni

Park has good facilities with aquarium, kids park, boating, small birds and animals etc.. good to have fun with family and friends. Public transportation is available from Theni.

5.Veerapandi Gowmariamman Devi Temple

The famous yearly Chithirai Gowmariamman festival is celebrated in this town for 8 days . The goddess in this temple is a Suyambu, which means it is not a man made one, it is found naturally while digging out and so this goddess is considered to be more powerful.

6.Kuchanur Sri Saneeswarar Temple

In this temple Lord Sani is present in splendid isolation bereft of the other planets. Here he is known as Kuchanooran.This is the only temple in India which is mean solely for Saneeswara Bhagawan situated at Kuchanoor, 20 Km south of Theni.

7.Top Station View Point

For bike rides, it's one of the best place. Great roads. Lots of places to see on the way. Very easily accessible.You don't have to Trek or anything. Better to visit early in the morning or in the evening..

8.Vaigai Dam

Vaigai Dam is also a fantastic destination for picnics and casual afternoon visits with friends and family. The dam also provides drinking water to Madurai and Andipatti. Owing to its 174,000,000 cubic metre capacity...

9. Meghamalai - High Wavy Mountains

It is a cool and misty mountain range situated in the Western Ghats in Theni district, Tamilnadu, South India. It is a place of natural beauty dotted with cardamom plantations and tea estates.

10.Southpparai Dam

It is one of the remote and untouched dam and it has retained its beauty. It is very scenic and greenery place.It is surrounded naturally by mountains. It gives you a fresh feeling as the water just enters from western ghats. It is a MUST place if you go to Theni.

11.Shanmuga
nathi Dam

Shanmuganathi Dam is a lifeline for the drylands to all agricultural villages of Theni. There is a Shanmugha Temple nearby within 1 kilometre, where resides an idol of Lord Murugan. You can visit that if you have time and pay your respects.

12.Kumbakarai falls

It offer shallow waters where tourists are welcomed to swim or to take a dip. The popular tourist spot is the most crowded during the rainy season when the water level is high and the neighboring areas glows with greenery. There is also a Murugan statue installed at the spot.
